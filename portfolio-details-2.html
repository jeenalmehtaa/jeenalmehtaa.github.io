About the project

I collaborated with a team of 3 members to build interactive dashboards that visualized attrition trends, layoff impacts and economic resilience across 12 sectors. This initiative can potentially lead to data-driven workforce planning. We integrated multiple datasets, transforming raw data into actionable insights.
