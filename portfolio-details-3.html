Project Information

  • Title: Chicago Car Crash Analysis
  • Domain: Data Science
  • Tech Stack: R, Statistics

About the project

Along with my team, I analyzed 740K traffic incidents in Chicago, informing targeted intersection redesigns that mitigated 28% of right-angle collisions. We predicted crash severity with 85% accuracy. We devised vehicle design modifications and put forth optimized emergency response plans that has the potential to reduce intersection-related car accidents.
